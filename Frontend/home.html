Share expenses, not headaches

PayShift is a user-friendly web application that simplifies the process of splitting expenses and managing shared finances. With PayShift, users can easily track expenses, settle debts, and view financial reports. The app allows for creating groups for shared expenses, adding bills and payments, and automating calculations for each person's share. Whether you're splitting bills with roommates or managing finances with family members or partners, PayShift makes it easy to stay on top of your shared finances.
